FAQ

Parking can get quite busy, so we recommend taking public transit, walking, or biking. We’re located on the #17 Oak bus route and a 15-minute walk from the Oakridge Canada Line station. Parking is free in our lot off West 37th avenue but spaces are limited. Free street parking is also available in the surrounding area (where street signs permit). Please respect our neighbourhood and driveways of our neighbours.

November and December:10:00am - 3:00pm Garden closed December 25.
Note: Last entry is half an hour before closing.

Working guide and service dogs are welcome. Leave pets at home.

Bicycles, scooters, and skateboards are not permitted in the garden. Strollers and wheelchairs are welcome.
